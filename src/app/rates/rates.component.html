The monthly relief as changed from KES 1,408 to KES 2,400.

Section 22C of the Income Tax Act as been deleted.

The section entitled any person who deposits funds to a registered home ownership savings plan (HOSP) to a deduction of up to KES 48,000 per year or KES 4,000 per month. The section also exempted from tax any interest income earned by a depositor on deposits of up to KES 3million.
